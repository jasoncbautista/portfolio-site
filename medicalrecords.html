Project: Signature Capture

In order to be able to collect records on behalf of a patient, authorization forms must be completed for each facility. This step is often cumbersome and tedious so a new system was designed to help reduce the time it took to complete this process.

The approach taken was to give users different options to sign digitally. An option to sign on a phone and then resume the onboarding process on a computer was eventually implemented. The reason to be able to sign on a phone was that it is more natural to do this. On the other hand, it is a bit tedious to fill out medical information on a phone so the user is given the option to return to their computer to finish inputting all their information.
